====== Puntuated Equiibrium ======
Created Tuesday 31 January 2023
@GEOLOGY @paleontology  @YEAR4

@Punctuated_equilibrium is a theory that a species, once established, will become stable and not change very much in its geological history, until rare and rapid geological events.
